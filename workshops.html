The Art of Empathy: A 4-Week Parent & Child Journey

This isn't just another activity class. It's a shared experience designed to strengthen your bond while equipping your child with the most important superpower of all: empathy. Through guided storytelling, collaborative art projects, and mindful exercises, you will both embark on a journey of emotional discovery and connection.

What You'll Both Discover:

  • ✓ The language of feelings: Naming and navigating emotions.
  • ✓ Creative expression through storytelling and collaborative art.
  • ✓ Mindful listening and communication exercises.
  • ✓ Building a 'Kindness Project' to take out into the world.
